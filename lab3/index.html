They’re the childhood friend that knows your highest highs and lowest lows. They’ve been with you through thick and thin and they’re the best at keeping secrets. Whether it’s dressed up or informal, cheeseburgers have your back.

They say you’re never supposed to meet your idols. In this case, it’s a good thing your idol is inanimate. Cheeseburgers are never going to give you up or let you down. They’ll also never run around or desert you. They’ll never make you cry, unless it’s from sheer joy. Cheeseburgers certainly won’t say goodbye either. Finally, cheeseburgers are incapable of telling lies or hurting you. On the flip side, they can still Rick Roll you.
